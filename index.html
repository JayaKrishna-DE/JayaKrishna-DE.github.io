About Me

I am a Senior Data Engineer with 9+ years of hands-on experience across the full Software Development Life Cycle — from requirements gathering and system design through to deployment, performance tuning, and production support of large-scale distributed data systems.

My expertise spans the entire Hadoop ecosystem (Hive, Spark, Kafka, HDFS, Sqoop, Oozie, Flume, NiFi), cloud platforms (Microsoft Azure and AWS), and modern ETL/ELT pipelines that process terabytes of data daily.

I have led Data Warehouse migrations from on-premises infrastructure to Microsoft Azure at Wells Fargo and Walmart, architected real-time streaming pipelines using Kafka and Spark Streaming, and built custom ingest frameworks powering business-critical analytics.

I bring strong skills in Python, Scala, and PySpark for both batch and streaming workloads, and hands-on experience with Power BI and Tableau for data visualization. I thrive in Agile/Scrum environments and have a consistent track record of delivering on time.

Currently at Wells Fargo (April 2024 – Present) — designing and deploying end-to-end Azure data solutions including ADF pipelines, HBase clusters, Kafka streaming, and on-premises to cloud migration.
